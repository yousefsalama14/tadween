who we are

A digital commercial electronic platform (B 2 B) that documents and facilitates billing and financial collections between wholesalers, suppliers / distributors, collectors on the one hand, and retailers on the other hand, by automating billing processes between the two parties, documenting collections and debts, and analyzing data simultaneously and easily, in addition to Monitor the performance of distribution and sales representatives.

It was established with the aim of finding smart and affordable solutions to fill the gaps in the market related to billing business between wholesalers and retailers, and to achieve optimal use of data through analytical reports.
